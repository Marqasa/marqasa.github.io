About Me

I have a long history of working with computers and have always found them interesting. I first got into programming using BASIC on a BBC Microcomputer and have dabbled with it ever since. I recently decided to focus on development full-time as I think it is a very valuable skill to have and will continue to become even more important in the future. To gain formal skills, I have recently graduated from a coding Traineeship as a full stack developer including completing 2 real-world projects successfully to specification using a wide range of programming languages. I am a good communicator and work well in a team or as a team leader. I am a keen learner and I don't get frustrated or give up easily. I am also a very good problem solver and pride myself on achieving high-quality results. I look forward to applying my skills and working on challenging and rewarding commercial projects.
